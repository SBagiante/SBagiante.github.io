Welcome!

I'm Ms.Bagiante, a passionate educator dedicated to making a positive, inclusive, and engaging learning environment for all my students. With a background in Technological and Applies Studies (TAS), I strive to inspire and support learners through innovative teaching methods and a commitment to promoting critical thinking and creativity.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of working in diverse classroom settings and developing tailored lesson plans. This portfolio showcases my journey as an educator, my teaching philosophy, sample lesson plans, and more!

Feel free to explore the site and learn more about my work, my passion for education, and my commitment to lifelong learning.
