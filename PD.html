AIS Teacher Trainee

As a teacher trainee at Minarah College in Green Valley, I am actively involved with the AIS Teaching School Hubs. Through this program, I participate in professional development meetings organised by AIS that align directly with my teaching degree, allowing me to apply new strategies and insights directly in the classroom.

Higher Order Education Tutoring

In addition to my training at Minarah College, I work with Higher Order Education as a private tutor for Math and English. Here, I tutor four different students, honing my teaching skills, and developing tailored resources to meet their individual learning needs. This experience provides me with valuable opportunities to practice and refine my instructional techniques in a one-on-one setting.
